About us

Our Uniquely Sarees, are exactly that, unique!... Each piece, meticulously designed, impeccably finished to give you sarees, found nowhere else... Truly designer, undoubtedly yours... Taking the best every fabric has to offer, the absolute best every weaving, blockprinting and handpainting tradition offers, taking the best karigari, to deliver you a piece truly one-of-a-kind.

A patchwork of fabrics and patterns, artistically manipulated, effortlessly assembled into one 6 yard masterpiece... To reflect, alongside a reverent respect for the heritage artforms we work with, a desire to stand out, in artful uniqueness, an exuberant need to innovate...create... especially, bespoke for you...
